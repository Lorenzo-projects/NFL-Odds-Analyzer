Understanding the Stats

Probability

The implied probability of an outcome, based on the average odds from all bookmakers. Higher probability suggests a more likely outcome.

Average Odds

The weighted average of the odds for a particular outcome across all available bookmakers. This provides a central estimate of the odds.

Consensus

A measure of agreement among bookmakers, indicating how closely their odds align. Higher consensus suggests stronger agreement.

Bookmakers

The number of bookmakers offering odds for a particular outcome. A higher number indicates more market interest and potentially more reliable data.
